Local-Only Philosophy

Your data belongs to you. Period.

1. Zero Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. The extension handles data strictly to function:

  • Website Content: When you save a bookmark, we store the URL and page title.
  • Web History: We access the active tab's metadata only when you interact with the extension.

2. Local Storage Only

All bookmarks and categories are stored locally in your browser's chrome.storage.local. No data is ever transmitted to a remote server or third party.

3. Complete Control

You have full control over your data. Delete individual bookmarks or categories anytime. Uninstalling automatically wipes all locally stored data.
